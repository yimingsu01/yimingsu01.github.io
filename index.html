Yiming Su

B.Sc. in Computer Science, The University of Chicago

Introduction

Yiming is a first-year Computer Science PhD at The University of Illinois-Urbana Champaign. He currently works with Prof. Tianyin Xu on cloud systems reliability. In the past, he had been working with Prof. Shan Lu on Software Reliability projects, such as bug analysis and bug finding. He is most proud of his work as the first author HotGPT: How to Make Software Documentation More Useful with a Large Language Model?, which is accepted in HotOS '23.
